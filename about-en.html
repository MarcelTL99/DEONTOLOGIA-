Traducciones MT

I am Marcel Tyler, a freelance translator specialising in the legal field, although I am open to other types of translation. At Traducciones MT we have a team that works with me on all types of economic, general and technical texts. You can consult all of our services, from legal translation to police interpreting. Although our main focus is on legal texts, we are available for any translation or interpreting needs.

I have a BA in Translation and Interpreting (Spanish, English and German) and a Master's degree in Institutional Translation. I have applied my knowledge in various fields, which has allowed me to build a team to deal with assignments beyond my abilities.

If you have any enquiries or require a specialised translation, please do not hesitate to contact us - we are here to help you!
